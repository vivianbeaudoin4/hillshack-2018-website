HILLS HACK

Saturday June 9th, 2018

8:00am - 10:00pm

HillsHack 2018 is Warren Hills Regional High School's 3rd annual hackathon!

Hosted by the computer science club at Warren Hills (CS@WH), HillsHack gives you the opportunity to work with your friends to learn more about technology and computer science and create your own hack! A hack is a cool project using computer science, some examples are:

  • a functional website
  • a mobile phone app
  • your own video game

Come and join us on June 9th to learn something new!
